Admission process

Only completely filled Registration forms will be accepted with the following documents:

  1. Two recent passport size photographs
  2. Copy of Birth Certificate (Nursery to standard 1st)
  3. Residence Proof (Local)
  4. Copy of Mark Sheet/Progress Report of the last class passed (Not for Nursery)
  5. Having a Certificate in original (Std. 2nd and above) signed by D.E.O. or authority of the board to which last attended school was affiliated.Copy of Caste Certificate
