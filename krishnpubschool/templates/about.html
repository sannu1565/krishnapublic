About us

Krishna School is a beacon of educational excellence, dedicated to nurturing young minds and empowering them to reach their full potential. With a rich history of academic achievement and a commitment to holistic development, Krishna School provides a supportive and inspiring learning environment. Our passionate educators employ innovative teaching methods and a well-rounded curriculum that not only fosters academic success but also instills values and critical thinking skills. At Krishna School, we believe in cultivating future leaders who are not just knowledgeable but also compassionate, creative, and ready to embrace the challenges of a rapidly changing world. Join us on a journey of discovery and growth, where each student's unique talents are celebrated, and their dreams are encouraged to flourish.

Our Vision

At Krishna Public School, we envision a world where every student is empowered with knowledge and skills, capable of contributing positively to society and becoming a responsible global citizen. We aim to provide a holistic education that goes beyond textbooks, fostering creativity, critical thinking, and a love for learning.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower students with a strong educational foundation, instill values, and help them discover their true potential. We are dedicated to:

1 Academic Excellence: We strive to provide a curriculum that is both challenging and enriching, ensuring that our students achieve their academic goals.

2 Character Development: We believe in the importance of values such as integrity, empathy, and respect, and we work to instill these qualities in our students

3 Holistic Growth: Our approach to education encompasses not only academics but also extracurricular activities, sports, and arts to develop well-rounded individuals.

4 Global Perspective: We aim to nurture an international outlook, enabling our students to thrive in a globalized world.

Our Faculty

Our modern and well-equipped campus provides a conducive environment for learning. Our facilities include well-maintained classrooms, science and computer labs, a library, a sports complex, and spaces for cultural and artistic activities.
